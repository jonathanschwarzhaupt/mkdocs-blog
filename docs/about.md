## What I Do
As a Data Engineer based in Munich, I architect and implement end-to-end data solutions with a focus on continuous improvement and automation. My role spans from setting up cloud infrastructure and designing data pipelines to optimizing workflows and ensuring data quality through automated monitoring and observability.
I'm particularly experienced with Microsoft Azure, where I architect and deploy cloud resources ranging from data lakes to analytics services. I'm also a Microsoft Certified [Azure Data Engineer](https://learn.microsoft.com/api/credentials/share/en-us/JonathanSchwarzhaupt-5816/A432492773D7B593?sharingId=18F3986EE2A538EB) and [Azure Administrator](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/azure-administrator/?practice-assessment-type=certification)!

I work extensively with both cloud-native services and open source tools, believing that the right solution often combines the best of both worlds. My approach is always guided by focusing the technical solution on business requirements and to iterate and improve systems over time. I believe the best data infrastructure evolves with the business it serves.
